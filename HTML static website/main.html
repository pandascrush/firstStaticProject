Detail About COVID-19

Overview. Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus. Most people infected with the virus will experience mild to moderate respiratory illness and recover without requiring special treatment. However, some will become seriously ill and require medical attention

Symptoms

Symptoms COVID-19 affects different people in different ways. Most infected people will develop mild to moderate illness and recover without hospitalization.

Most common symptoms:

  1. fever
  2. cough
  3. tiredness
  4. loss of taste or smell

    5. Less common symptoms:

  5. sore throat
  6. headache
  7. aches and pains
  8. diarrhoea
  9. a rash on skin, or discolouration of fingers or toes red or irritated eyes

    10. Serious symptoms:

  10. difficulty breathing or shortness of breath
  11. loss of speech or mobility
  12. confusion chest pain

Seek immediate medical attention if you have serious symptoms. Always call before visiting your doctor or health facility.
